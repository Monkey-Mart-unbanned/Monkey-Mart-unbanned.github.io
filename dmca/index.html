DMCA Notice

Last updated: June 5, 2026

Monkey Mart respects copyright. The games on this site are embedded from third-party hosts, but if you own a copyright and you think something here is using your work without permission, send us a takedown notice and we'll act on it. This page walks through what a valid notice needs to include.

What to include in your notice

To be valid under the DMCA, your notice should have all of the following:

  • Your full name, organization (if any), mailing address, phone number, and email address.
  • A description of the copyrighted work you say was infringed. If it covers multiple works, list them all.
  • The exact URL or location of the material you want removed or disabled. URLs are far more useful than page names.
  • A statement, in your own words, that you have a good-faith belief the use isn't authorized by the copyright owner, its agent, or the law.
  • A statement that the information in your notice is accurate and, under penalty of perjury, that you are the copyright owner or authorized to act for the owner.
  • Your physical or electronic signature.

Counter-notices

If we took something down that you think was a mistake, you can send a counter-notice. It should include your contact info, a description of the material and where it was before we removed it, a statement under penalty of perjury that you have a good-faith belief the removal was a mistake, and your consent to the jurisdiction of a federal court in your area. We'll forward valid counter-notices to the original complainant.

What we do with valid notices

We read every notice that comes in. If it's complete and the claim looks legitimate, we remove or disable access to the material and let the person who posted it know (if we can find them). For repeat infringers, we may need to lock down parts of the site or take other reasonable steps.

Send your notice

Open an issue on the GitHub repository that hosts Monkey Mart, or use the contact method listed on the homepage. Include all the items from the list above so we can act on it the first time.
