How to play Game of Farmers

Playing Game of Farmers is simple. Click a plot to plant the first seed, water the plant when it needs it, and harvest the crop when it is ready. Sell the produce at the market, then spend the earnings on more plots, better seeds, and helpers that automate the work. The more you sell, the more the farm grows, and the more the farm grows, the more you can automate.

Tips and tricks

The best Game of Farmers strategy is to keep the plots full. An empty plot does not grow, and a ready crop left standing does not earn. Hire helpers as soon as you can afford them, so you can focus on the highest-margin produce. Unlock the most expensive seed you can afford early, because profit scales with the price of the crop. Magical seeds are the late game, but the early unlocks still pay well enough to set up the farm and start automating the routine.
