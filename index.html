How to play Monkey Mart

Playing Monkey Mart is straightforward. Start by planting crops in the field. Wait for them to grow, then harvest them. Take the harvested goods to the processing area to turn them into products. Stock those products on the shelves. Customers will walk in and buy them. Use the money you earn to buy more seeds, unlock new products, and upgrade your equipment. As you expand, you can hire helpers who will plant, harvest, stock, and serve for you. The goal is to build a mart that runs smoothly from farming to checkout.

Monkey Mart tips and tricks

Good Monkey Mart runs usually start with attention to the basics. Plant crops early and keep the field running. Process goods as soon as they are harvested so shelves stay full. Watch which products sell fastest and focus on those. Upgrade equipment when you have the cash because faster processing means more sales. Hire helpers when the store gets busy so you can focus on expansion. The game rewards steady growth. A rushed mart with empty shelves makes less money than a small mart that keeps customers happy.
