How to play Mini Monkey Mart

Playing Mini Monkey Mart is simple. Move the monkey over the register to take payments, then dash to the shelves to restock them. Sell the bananas, then spend the earnings on new products, helpers, and upgrades. The loop is shorter than the original, so the first expansion lands faster and the late game kicks in earlier.

Tips and tricks

The best Mini Monkey Mart strategy is to keep moving. Never let a shelf sit empty, because an empty shelf does not sell. Hire helpers as soon as you can afford them, so you can focus on the highest-margin products. Unlock the doggo helper early, because restocking is the biggest time sink. After the first expansion, prioritize the most expensive product you can afford, since the profit per click scales with the price of the item.
