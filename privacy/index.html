Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 5, 2026

Monkey Mart hosts free browser games like Monkey Mart and Bunny Market. This page explains what we collect, why we collect it, and what the third-party services on this site do with your data. The short version: we don't ask for your name, email, or any account, and the only thing we store on your device is your favorites list.

What we collect

You can play every game on Monkey Mart without signing up. We don't collect names, emails, or account info. The only thing we save on your device is a small list of games you've favorited, stored in your browser's local storage so it shows up the next time you visit.

Third-party services running on this site may collect technical data on their own. That includes things like your browser type, device, screen size, pages visited, approximate location from your IP, and how you interact with the page. We use this to keep the site working and to figure out which games people actually play.

What we use it for

The data goes toward keeping the site online, remembering your favorites, measuring traffic, fixing broken pages, blocking abuse, and serving ads where they appear. We don't sell personal info because we don't have any to sell.

Cookies and local storage

We use cookies and local storage to remember your preferences and to let third-party analytics and ad networks do their thing. You can turn off cookies in your browser settings, but that will also wipe your saved favorites. Most modern browsers also have a "Do Not Track" or "Global Privacy Control" option, which we honor where required by law.

Third-party services

The games themselves are loaded from third-party hosts. We link to game files and embed them in iframes, but we don't run those servers. Each game has its own privacy policy, and you'll want to check those if you have specific concerns. Analytics and ads come from outside services too, and they have their own rules about data.

Children

This site is built for a general audience. We don't target kids under 13 and we don't knowingly collect data from them. If you're a parent and you think your child ended up sharing something with one of our third-party services, get in touch and we'll help sort it out.

Changes to this policy

We update this page when something changes. The date at the top tells you when it was last touched. If we make a big change, we'll try to make it obvious on the homepage too.

Contact

Got a privacy question? Reach out through the GitHub repository that hosts this site, and we'll get back to you as soon as we can.
